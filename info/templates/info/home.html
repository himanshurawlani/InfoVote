About Company


Infovote was conceived from the need for accountability and transparency in governance, and the desire to vote informed. We want to connect the citizens of our nation to their electoral candidates and chosen representatives to facilitate grassroots level implementation and problem solving.


Through our website and application we provide web-users with essential candidate, political party and constituency data on a mobile platform, available anytime, anywhere. We also provide a medium for electoral candidates and representatives holding office to create their profile which lists their activities, opinions, achievements as well as enables them to carry out surveys and feedback collection projects.


Our Values


MISSION: By helping everyone voice their opinion and making sure representatives listen, we can help make governance effective and help voters - vote informed.


VISION: Our uniqueness lies in collecting online feedback on candidate profiles; work of elected representatives; national policies and local issues from our users. This valuable hyperlocal user data analysis along with a digital campaign profile will help leaders and parties choose candidates, policies and undertake activities & implement solutions that benefit citizens.
